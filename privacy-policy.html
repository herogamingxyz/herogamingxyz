Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of:

  • 🌐 The Hero Verse website (herogamingxyz.github.io)

Data We Collect

We collect different types of data depending on how you interact with Hero Verse:

Data Type What We Collect When
👤 Account Info Username, email address, join date, profile photo On signup / profile edit
🔒 Auth Data Encrypted password hash (via Firebase Auth) On account creation

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Hero Verse experience. Here's exactly what we do with it:

  • 🔑 Authentication: To verify your identity when you log in and keep your account secure.
  • 🎮 Platform Features: To show your profile, track mod uploads, display download counts, and enable creator tools.
  • 📧 Communications: To send important account notifications (password resets, security alerts). We do not send unsolicited marketing emails.
  • 📊 Analytics: To understand which mods, articles, and features are most popular — helping us make Hero Verse better.
  • 🛡️ Security: To detect and prevent spam, abuse, unauthorized access, and fraudulent activity.
  • ⚖️ Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and respond to lawful requests from authorities if required.
💡 We use Google Analytics with anonymized IPs. This means we can see traffic patterns but cannot identify individual users from analytics data.

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share data in the following limited circumstances:

  • 🔥 Firebase (Google): Our backend for authentication and database. Firebase processes your account data under Google's strict privacy standards.
  • 📊 Google Analytics: Anonymized usage statistics to understand platform traffic. No personally identifiable information is sent.
  • ⚖️ Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our users' safety.
  • 🔄 Business Transfer: If Hero Verse is acquired or merged, user data may transfer as part of that transaction with advance notice to users.
⚠️ Third-party links (Discord, YouTube, Amazon affiliate products) have their own privacy policies. Hero Verse is not responsible for external sites' data practices.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • 🔐 All passwords are hashed and encrypted via Firebase Authentication — we never store plain-text passwords.
  • 🌐 All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • 🏛️ Firebase Firestore security rules restrict unauthorized access to user data.
  • 👁️ Regular security reviews are conducted to identify and patch vulnerabilities.
  • 🚨 In the unlikely event of a data breach, affected users will be notified within 72 hours.
🔒 While we do our best to protect your data, no internet system is 100% secure. We encourage using a strong, unique password and enabling secure authentication where possible.

Firebase & Google Services

Hero Verse is built on Google Firebase, which powers our authentication, database, and storage systems. The following Firebase services are used:

  • 🔑 Firebase Authentication: Manages login, signup, and session security.
  • 🗄️ Cloud Firestore: Stores user profiles, mod data, and article content.
  • 📊 Google Analytics: Tracks anonymized usage and traffic patterns.

Firebase data is stored on Google's secure cloud servers. Google's data processing terms apply. For more info, visit firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

ℹ️ Firebase servers are GDPR compliant and SOC 2 certified, meeting international data security standards.
