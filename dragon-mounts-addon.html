🐉 Dragon Mounts Addon

Ride powerful dragons, hatch dragon eggs and explore Minecraft Bedrock like never before.

⚠️ This addon is discontinued and may not work properly on newer Minecraft Bedrock versions.

📖 About Dragon Mounts Addon

Dragon Mounts Addon adds 17 unique dragons to Minecraft Bedrock Edition. Players can tame, ride and fly dragons while exploring the world. Each dragon has unique abilities, attacks, armor and special powers. Some dragons breathe fire, some shoot ice, while others control lightning and magic.

🔥 Main Features

  • 🐉 17 Unique Dragons
  • ⚔️ Dragon Armor & Weapons
  • 🔥 Fire Dragons
  • ❄️ Ice Dragons
  • ⚡ Lightning Dragons
  • 👻 Ghost Dragons
  • 🌊 Water Dragons
  • ☀️ Sunlight Dragon
  • 🌙 Moonlight Dragon
  • ✈️ Fly & Ride Dragons

🐉 Dragon Types

The addon includes many dragon species such as Aether Dragon, Fire Dragon, Ender Dragon, Ghost Dragon, Water Dragon, Lightning Dragon, Skeleton Dragon, Wither Dragon, Zombie Dragon, Enchanted Dragon, Ice Dragon, Terra Dragon, Forest Dragon, Taiga Dragon, Nether Dragon, Sunlight Dragon and Moonlight Dragon.

⚔️ Dragon Powers

Every dragon comes with unique abilities. Fire Dragons shoot fireballs, Ice Dragons slow enemies, Lightning Dragons strike powerful attacks and Ghost Dragons launch explosive projectiles. Some dragons can reach over 1000 health making them extremely powerful companions.

✈️ Flying System

The addon includes a flying feature. Simply ride your dragon and: ⬆️ Look up to fly higher ⬇️ Look down to descend This allows players to travel across their Minecraft worlds from the sky.
